# 🥩 Beef Wellington Recipe

An elegant and flavorful centerpiece made with beef tenderloin, mushroom duxelles, and flaky puff pastry.

---

## 📝 Ingredients

### 🥩 For the Wellington:
- 1kg beef tenderloin (center-cut)
- Salt and black pepper
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp English mustard (or Dijon)

### 🍄 For the Duxelles:
- 500g mushrooms (cremini or mixed), finely chopped
- 2 shallots (minced)
- 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp thyme leaves
- Salt and pepper

### 🥓 For Wrapping:
- 6–8 slices prosciutto
- 1 sheet puff pastry (or enough to wrap completely)
- 1 egg yolk (beaten, for egg wash)

---

## 👩‍🍳 Instructions

### 1. **Prepare the Beef**
1. Season beef with salt and pepper.
2. Sear on all sides in hot oil (2–3 min per side). Let cool.
3. Brush with mustard. Let rest.

### 2. **Make the Duxelles**
1. In a pan, melt butter and sauté shallots and garlic until soft.
2. Add mushrooms and thyme. Cook until all moisture evaporates and mixture is paste-like (about 15–20 min).
3. Season with salt and pepper. Cool completely.

### 3. **Assemble**
1. Lay prosciutto slices overlapping on plastic wrap.
2. Spread duxelles over the prosciutto.
3. Place the beef on top. Roll tightly using plastic wrap. Chill for 30 minutes.

### 4. **Wrap in Pastry**
1. Roll out puff pastry on floured surface.
2. Remove plastic wrap and place beef in center.
3. Fold and seal the pastry. Chill again for 15–20 minutes.
4. Brush with egg yolk. Optionally score top with knife for decoration.

---

## 🔥 Bake

- Preheat oven to **200°C (390°F)**.
- Bake for **35–45 minutes** or until:
  - Internal temp: **50–52°C (122–125°F)** for medium-rare.
- Let rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing.

---

## 🍷 Serving Suggestions

- Serve with:
  - Roasted potatoes
  - Red wine reduction
  - Seasonal vegetables

---

## 🔁 Tips

- Use a meat thermometer to avoid overcooking.
- Chill at each step to help maintain shape.
- Substitute prosciutto with crepes for a non-pork version.
